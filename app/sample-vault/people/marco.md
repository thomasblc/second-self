# Marco

My main collaborator on [[projects/second-self-app]]. Marco handles the model side: the fine-tuning recipe, the base-model choice, the evaluation. I handle the product and the vault.

How we work: I tend to over-structure, he tends to over-build. He thinks my [[ideas/atomic-notes]] habit is overhead, I think his "just write a big doc" habit loses the links. The friction is useful. Most of our good decisions came from one of us pushing back hard on the other.

Things Marco is usually right about:
- Ship the small model honestly instead of waiting for the big one.
- Measure on real data before believing any benchmark.
- Retrieval first, fine-tuning second, because facts matter more than style for most questions.
