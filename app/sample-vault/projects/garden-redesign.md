# Garden redesign

The balcony gets hard afternoon sun and almost nothing in the morning, so anything that wants gentle light slowly cooks. The redesign is really a light-budget problem disguised as a plant problem.

Plan for this season:
- Move the herbs to the east rail where they get the soft morning hours.
- Give the west rail to things that actually want the heat: tomatoes, peppers, rosemary.
- Add a slatted screen so the 3pm sun is filtered, not blocked.
